How to Play ⚡

🎮 Objective

Navigate a 15×15 grid and dodge infection while stacking up points.

🚶 Movement

Use arrow keys to move around the grid. Chat around, vibe with other players.

🦠 Infection

Get close to red players? You’re infected (up, down , left, right, diagonals)! Once infected, spread the chaos. Freeze enemies with power-ups.

⚔️ Power-ups

✦ Shield protects you. ❄ Freeze stuns infected. ★ Star boosts score. ⚠︎ Red Wall makes you infected.

🏆 Scoring

Stay healthy, collect power-ups, flex your high score. Top scorer wins!
